About Us

Provider of the Best Medical Supplies and Equipment Needed for Your Care.

Welcome to Concor Medical Supplies and Equipment!

Concor Medical Supplies and Equipment is a family-owned business that started in 2018. The company is owned and managed by four siblings, whose family's main business was also in the medical field. One of the siblings is currently studying a medicine-related course.

Our mission is to be a trusted partner in the healthcare industry by offering a comprehensive range of medical supplies and equipment that meet the needs of our customers. Whether you are looking for surgical instruments, diagnostic equipment, respiratory care products, or any other medical supplies, we have you covered.

We work with leading manufacturers to ensure that our products are of the highest quality and meet the latest industry standards. Our team of experts is always available to answer any questions you may have and provide guidance on selecting the right products for your needs.

At Concor, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service, competitive pricing, and timely delivery. We understand that every customer has unique requirements, and we strive to provide personalized solutions that meet your specific needs.
